\begin{sol}
    We can rewrite the equation as:
    \begin{equation}
        \bff{A}^2 = -\bff{1}
    \end{equation}
    Taking the determinant of both sides:
    \begin{equation}
        \det(\bff{A})^2 = (-1)^n
    \end{equation}
    and therefore:
    \begin{equation}
        \det \bff{A} = \sqrt{(-1)^n}
    \end{equation}
    If $n$ is even, then $\det \bff{A} = 1$. Otherwise, $\det \bff{A} = i$ where $i$ is the imaginary number.
\end{sol}
